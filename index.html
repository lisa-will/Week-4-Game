Crystal Collector Game

How the Game Works:

At the start of the game you will be given a random number!

The six crystals below have been assigned a mystery number. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score or "Crystal Count".

You can only win the game by matching your Crystal Count to the random number. If your Crystal Count is higher than the random number you lose the game.

Keep in mind, each time the game restarts the value of each crystal also changes! The crystal's value are never the same. So keep a watchful eye and perhaps a calculator handy!
