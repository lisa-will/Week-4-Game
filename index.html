CRSYTAL COLLECTOR!

How to Play:

At the start of the game you will be given a random number.

The six crystals below have been given a mystery number. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You only win the game by matching your total score to the random number. If your total score is higher than the random number you lose the game.

Each time the game restarts, so does the value of each crystal! The crystals points are never the same. So keep a watchfull eye and watch out for crystal bandits!
